About Trend Radar

Trend Radar is an AI-powered intelligence platform that monitors, analyzes, and predicts internet trends in real-time. We cut through the noise to deliver actionable insights on what's genuinely capturing the world's attention.

🔍

Real-Time Monitoring

Our system continuously scans Google Trends, social media platforms, and news sources to identify emerging trends before they go mainstream. We update every few minutes to ensure you never miss what's next.

🤖

AI-Powered Analysis

Using Claude AI, we analyze each trend's context, sentiment, and trajectory. Our AI determines whether a trend is rising, peaking, or fading — and predicts its potential longevity.

📊

Signal Scoring

Every trend receives a proprietary Signal Score (0-100) based on search momentum, social engagement, and cross-platform presence. Higher scores indicate stronger, more authentic viral potential.

🌐

Multi-Platform Tracking

We monitor X (Twitter), TikTok, Instagram, and Google to give you a complete picture of where trends are gaining traction. Different platforms often signal different audience demographics.

📈

Lifecycle Analysis

Trends go through stages: New → Rising → Peak → Stable → Fading → Dead. We track this lifecycle in real-time so you know exactly when to engage and when to move on.

🪙

Crypto & Meme Coins

When relevant, we surface associated meme coins and tokens via DexScreener. We only show tokens when there's genuine market activity — no pump-and-dump promotions, just raw data.
